About Us

KIDDO HUB

Kiddo HUB is a National initiative dedicated to identifying, nurturing, and promoting children's talents worldwide Starting from Rwanda. We provide platforms where talents of young children aged 3-15 can be showcased, developed, and celebrated. These talents range from leadership, music, dance, drama, fashion, comedy/theater, Poetry/Literary arts, Playing Piano and Guitar, Visual Arts (Painting, Sculpture), Basic technology (Graphic designs) among others. The Hub is also meant to boost parenting campaigns, joyful Teaching-learning in Nursery and primary, Educational tours, cultural education all aspiring to prepare children to thrive in a globalized world while staying rooted in their heritage.
